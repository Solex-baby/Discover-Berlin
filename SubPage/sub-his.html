Tracing Time: Walking Through Berlin’s Living History

Standing before the Berlin Wall, you can still feel the echoes of division and hope that shaped generations. Its colorful murals now speak of freedom and unity.

Climb to the glass dome of the Reichstag Building for breathtaking city views. It’s where Berlin’s past meets its forward-looking spirit, open to everyone.

Checkpoint Charlie — Echoes of the Cold War

At Checkpoint Charlie, history feels close enough to touch. Once a tense border, it now tells stories of escape, courage, and divided lives.

Step inside the Museum Haus am Checkpoint Charlie to see artifacts and real escape devices used during the Cold War.

Charlottenburg Palace — A Glimpse of Royal Berlin

Walk through the gardens of Charlottenburg Palace and imagine the grandeur of Prussian royalty surrounded by art and elegance.

Inside the Old Palace, every chandelier and portrait whispers stories of Berlin’s royal past and timeless beauty.

Berlin Cathedral — The City’s Spiritual Landmark

The Berlin Cathedral stands tall on Museum Island, a masterpiece of faith, architecture, and history at the city’s heart.

Climb up the dome for a panoramic view of the Spree Riverand the skyline that blends centuries of Berlin’s stories.
